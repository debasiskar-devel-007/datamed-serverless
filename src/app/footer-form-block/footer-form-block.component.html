The Pilot

DataMed Unlimited is now taking on country Pilots for our Global Humanitarian Healthcare Initiative. The relational objective is to significantly improve the ability to test, diagnose and treat the population of any country.

Through the ability to “take the diagnostics to the people,” DataMed Unlimited is able to provide detailed actionable data to the local Health Care System for coordination. Simply put, DataMed Unlimited is focused on scaling the ability to aggregate data and provide countries’ health system a better opportunity to improve health-related outcomes.
